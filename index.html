What is GEKKO?

GEKKO is a 3D-printed desktop robot that comes with a digital learning platform. It is designed to be robust and easily maintainable for repeated use.

It teaches robotics in the best way possible — through practical exercises and interactive lessons.

GEKKO App

The GEKKO robot works in tandem with the GEKKO App. The app offers a wide range of robotics topics, each featuring an exercise with GEKKO or a theory lesson.

Choose topics yourself and create a personally tailored study plan — from basic robot programming using GEKKO CS to building your own control system from scratch. The GEKKO App can teach it all.

GEKKO CS

Control and program GEKKO with GEKKO CS. The simple drag-and-drop system allows anyone to create precise and complex programs quickly and easily, with no prior experience.

Together, GEKKO CS and the GEKKO App provide the ultimate out-of-the-box experience.
